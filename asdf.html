ASDF Vector 

    ASDF Vector is a tool for visualizing sequence diagrams in the
    Augmented Sequence Diagram Format (ASDF).  Upload an ASDF file
    to view and interact with it.

    Release Notes - v1.0 (December 1, 2024)
    ---------------------------------------
    • Parse and visualize ASDF input.
    • View/peek additional signal information on click/hover, accordingly.
    • Hide, unhide and reorder participants as preferred.
    • Filter signals by hiding participants.
    • Highlight clicked signal and relations by coloring when needed.
    • Show/hide instance identifiers on signal arrows.
    • Colorize special signals in red.
    • Keep participant order and visibility over reloads, if reasonable.
    • Preserve toolbar settings in browser over page reloads.
    • Assign sequence numbers to signals.
    • Align window heights for diagram and additional info.

    Feature Requests
    ----------------
    • Show participants even if diagram's top is scrolled out of view.
    • Filter signals by time (interval) and additional info content.
    • Show signal list view on demand.
    • Step over subsequent signals (first, prev, next, last).
    • Resolve signal relations beyond neighbours.
    • Fold indentation blocks in additional info.
    • Show timestamps/deltas beside signal sequence numbers.
    • Render additional info header.
    • Preserve divider position over page reloads.
    • Color participant if sending message(s) to that fails.
